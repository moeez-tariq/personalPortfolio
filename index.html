My Personal Projects

SNACK KITCHEN

The aim of this project is to provide access to a variety of easy-to-make quick snacks that are healthy and can be made in the comfort of one's own dorm room. The website encourages students to take control of their diets and make healthier choices. The main star snack on the website is the date snack, which is not only healthy but also a part of the local culture.

CAPSTONE TOUR

The project is designed to be a one-stop-shop for all things Interactive Media. It offers a comprehensive overview of the Interactive Media program at NYUAD and showcases the incredible work of students. It explores the final showcase for the Capstone projects of NYUAD's Interactive Media students, taking place inside the 'Black Box' located on Campus in building C3.

INTERACTIVE COMIC

The project is a comic strip that explores the impact of plastic bottles on the environment. The overarching concept is to showcase how plastic bottles continue to linger around for years, adding to the existing garbage in the environment. The project achieves this by depicting the plastic bottle in the same location, while the rest of the city around it develops and changes over time.

GREEDY DOG

The purpose of this website was to allow the user to experience the story of a greedy dog who let go of the blessings in his life, in order to pursue more than what he needed. This project showcases the story in a unique way where the text is accompanied by six pictures. The website itself has scroll animation, making the website a fun little tool for young ones.
